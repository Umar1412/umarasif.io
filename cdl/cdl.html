The Call of Duty League is a professional esports league for the video game series Call of Duty, produced by its publisher Activision.


The CDL Consists of 12 Different Teams


The teams are: Optic Texas, Atlanta Faze, Toronto Ultra, Vegas Legion, Seattle Surge, New York Subliners, Los Angelos Thieves, Miami Heretics, Minnesota Rokkr, Los Angeles Guerrillas, Carolina Royal Ravens And Boston Breach.
