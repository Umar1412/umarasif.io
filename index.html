About Call Of Duty

Call of Duty is a first-person shooter video game series created by Infinity Ward. The series was published by Activision and most of the games have been developed by Infinity Ward and Treyarch, though some were developed by Amaze Entertainment and Gray Matter Interactive Studios.


As a first-person shooter, Call of Duty places the player in control of an infantry soldier who makes use of various authentic World War II firearms in combat.

Call Of Duty Gameplay And Popularity


Call of Duty is mostly casual. The reason why it's popular is because of the arcade gameplay.
